Input Guidelines

1. Use brackets to separate the different parts of the expression. (Recommended)

2. Two asteriskis are used for exponent as follows: 

x3 - x2 + 2  =  x**3 - x**2 + 2
x3 - 2x2 + x - 1  =  x**3 + 2x**2 + x - 1

3. Tolerable Error can be a float or in Scientific Notation such as:

0.0001   OR   10-E5   OR   10-e5
(All are correct)
